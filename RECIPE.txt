Hybrid Recipe for Paella

PAELLA

INGREDIENTS:
- 2 cups rice (ideally bomba)
- 2 cups fish stock
- Turmeric powder
- Sweet paprika

- Marinara mix
- Mussels, scrubbed and debearded.

- 1 red onion
- 5 cloves garlic
- Peas
- 1 can of diced tomatoes
- Red capsicum
- Parsley to garnish
- Lemon


METHOD:
1. Chop garlic, onion and capsicum
2. On medium heat put oil in pan add salt
3. Fry marinara mix. Remove and set aside.
4. Fry onion + garlic until fragrant
5. Add capsicum and fry. Stir every now and then until they soften.
6. Add diced tomatoes + peas
7. Add salt + black pepper + sweet paprika
8. Add marinara mix again
9. Add fish stock
10. Add turmeric and bring to high heat until boil.
11. Add rice all over evenly. Partly cover until the rice starts to cook. (5/10 mins)
12. Add mussels on top, pressing into mixture and close lid.
13. After 5 mins lower fire to low-medium. Simmer for 10 minutes or until rice is cooked.

FOR CRUSTY BASE
1. Raise to medium heat for 1 minute
2. Turn off heat
3. Cover and let rest for 5-7 minutes
4. Garnish with parsley and lemon

SERVE
